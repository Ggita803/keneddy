About THE REFORMIST

Akampurura Kennedy is a visionary leader dedicated to reforming broken systems at Kyambogo University. With a passion for change and a commitment to excellence, Kennedy brings fresh perspectives to student leadership.

From community engagement to academic advocacy, Kennedy has consistently demonstrated the qualities needed to lead the Guild effectively and represent all students fairly.
